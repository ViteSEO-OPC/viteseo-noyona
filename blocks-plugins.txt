Noyona Child Theme — Blocks and Plugins (Descriptions)

Blocks (source: /home/viteseo-dev/Local Sites/noyona_backup/noyona-childtheme/blocks)
- blog-list: Blog List — Curated blog list with filters and cards.
- blog-slide: Blog Slide — Related blogs carousel.
- blogs-view: Blogs View — Single post view with table of contents.
- brand-carousel: Brand / Reviews Carousel — Scrolling carousel for brands or top reviews.
- collection-grid: Collection Grid — Grid of collection cards with hover reveal for product count.
- color-swatches: Color Swatches — Row of product color swatches with optional labels.
- coming-soon: Coming Soon — Coming soon hero with email notify form.
- contact: Noyona Contact (Store Locator) — Store locator block with map and list.
- contact-form: Contact Form — Community contact form with info card and map.
- discover-posts-carousel: Discover Posts Carousel — Horizontal card carousel of recent blog posts.
- faq: FAQ — FAQ section with simple or dropdown layout.
- faq-list: FAQ List — FAQ list with categories, search, and accordion items.
- founder-section: Founder Section — Founder image, logo badge, copy and CTA button.
- hero-banner: Hero Banner — Wide hero banner with left-aligned text and background image.
- inquiry: Noyona Inquiry (FAQ) — Inquiry and FAQ block.
- location: Noyona Location (Store Locator) — Store locator block with map and list.
- mosaic-grid: Mosaic Grid — Mosaic grid of editorial tiles.
- not-found: 404 — 404 not found hero layout.
- phone-video-reviews: Phone Video Reviews — Row of iPhone-style frames containing YouTube videos.
- product-highlight: Product Highlight — Carousel of product cards with configurable visible count.
- product-slide: Product Slide — Carousel of best seller product cards.
- reviews-carousel: Top Reviews — Infinite scrolling carousel for customer reviews.
- search-expand: Search Expand — Search block that expands on click with live suggestions.
- terms: Terms — Terms & policy layout with table of contents.
- thank-you: Your Glow-Up is on the Way! — Thank-you hero section.

Plugins (source: /home/viteseo-dev/Local Sites/noyona_backup/noyona-childtheme/plugins)
- store-plugin: Store Manager Plugin — Registers a Store custom post type and adds admin meta boxes for location (map + lat/lng), opening/closing times, and a products repeater (name, category, description, quantity, image). Enqueues Leaflet and custom admin assets. Packaged as store-plugin/ and store-plugin.zip.
